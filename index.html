SUPERCHARGING SALES PERFORMANCE WITH ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Date: 15th July 2020, Time: 10:00AM - 11:00AM PST

REGISTER NOW

Salespeople are the frontline resources of any company. Given their proximity to customers, they must be empowered with actionable intelligence to be more responsive and to anticipate changes in buyer behaviors. Sadly, CRM platforms frequently overburden sales resources with administrative data collection demands, which, add no value to customers or the buying pro-cess. Artificial intelligence is changing this dynamic by boosting Sales Performance Manage-ment solutions, quickly analyzing massive volumes of structured and unstructured data, ena-bling pattern detection in forecasts, and recommending the next best steps for sales teams.

Join Enquero and Anaplan for a deep dive into how artificial intelligence can offer an incredible jump forward to Sales Performance Management and realign focus to solve complex use cases.

In this webinar, we will explore:

  • Why sales organizations must embed AI for better and faster decision making
  • How leveraging AI improves sales performance and strategy
  • How enabling data-driven sales performance ensures a faster response to changing customer behaviour
